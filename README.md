## The problem

Every launchpad makes you pick one token out of hundreds. And every launchpad dataset says the same thing: **the average buyer picks the wrong one.** Picking is the losing move.

## The idea

FIVEPACK removes the pick. Instead of one token, you buy **one pack** — a single token that holds **5 curated launches**, 20% each. You make one easy decision ("this week's edition: in or out?") instead of the hard one you keep getting wrong.

It's VC behavior — spread across many launches — in one click.

## How it works

| | Step | |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | **Curate** | Of everyone who applies, only **5** pass the editorial filter each week. Working demo, clean dev history, diverse categories. Getting in is the status. |
| **2** | **Drop** | Buy **1 pack token**, one transaction, gas sponsored. Funds route 20/20/20/20/20 to the five projects. Fair FCFS ordering — bots can't sweep it. |
| **3** | **Seal** | The pack lives **30 days** as one asset — tradeable whole, but not crackable. Live NAV shows who's carrying. Waiting is the feature. |
| **4** | **Unpack** | Burn the pack → receive all 5 tokens with a **gacha-style reveal**. Keep the winners, sell the duds. Share the result card. |

## Why people buy

- **You know you're bad at picking.** So do we. We removed the step.
- **Honest safety.** Not "guaranteed profit" — a promise math can keep: one rug won't end you.
- **The unpack is the moment.** Pack-opening with real assets inside — the most shareable format in crypto.
- **For small builders:** joining an edition = thousands of day-1 holders. Distribution no other channel gives you.
